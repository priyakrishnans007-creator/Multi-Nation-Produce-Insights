# Multi-Nation-Produce-Insights
Multi-Nation Produce Insights
A dynamic Excel dashboard project that analyzes global produce sales across multiple countries, categories, and time periods. This dataset-driven tool helps visualize trends, compare performance, and answer key business questions using Excel formulas and pivot tables.

Project Overview
This dashboard provides insights into:
- Sales performance by product, category, country, and month
- Total and average sales metrics
- Distribution of fruit vs vegetable sales
- Country-wise contribution to overall revenue
- Time-based sales trends

Dataset Summary
The dataset includes:
- Products: Apple, Banana, Mango, Orange, Brocoli, Carrots, Beans
- Categories: Fruit, Vegetables
- Countries: 10+ including United States, Canada, Germany, France, Australia, United Kingdom, New Zealand
- Date Range: 2016â€“2018
- Fields: Product, Category, Amount, Date, Country

   Dashboard Features
- Sales by Country & Category: Pivot table showing total sales split by fruit and vegetable categories across countries
- Sales by Product: Total sales amount per product
- Sales by Month: Aggregated monthly sales trends
- Sales by Category: Overall contribution of fruits vs vegetables
  
   Practice Questions Included
The workbook includes 10+ embedded questions for Excel learners:
- Count of Banana sales
- Total sales for Apple
- Total vegetable sales in France
- Percentage of Banana sales vs total
- Count of sales in Canada
- Average sale amount for Carrots in France
- Row number of first sale in France
- Number of unique countries
- Country of first Orange sale
- Category of first Mango entry

These questions are ideal for practicing:
- COUNTIFS, SUMIFS, AVERAGEIFS
- MATCH, INDEX, VLOOKUP
- Logical and conditional formulas
